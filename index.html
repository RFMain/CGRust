First Tournament!

2024/11/5

Crusader Games Network is excited to announce our first official tournament, running from Thursday, November 7th, 2024, at 7:00 PM CST to Sunday, November 10th, 2024, at 7:00 PM CST. This four-day event has been expertly organized by sRabbits and BigMac, and we extend our gratitude for their efforts in bringing this competition to life. With three teams vying for a prize pool of $150 awarded based on individual kills, this tournament promises to deliver a weekend of intense, high-stakes action. Further details, including registration information, are available on our Discord and in the official registration document. We look forward to seeing our community come together for this exciting inaugural event.
